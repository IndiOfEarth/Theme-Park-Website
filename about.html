About The Nexus!

Learn about our mission to inspire wonder and adventure through thrilling rides, immersive experiences, and educational programs. Discover the magic of the universe with us!

We've come a long way, and we're just getting started.

Origins

Inspired by the incredible achievements of space exploration and the boundless mysteries of the cosmos, our founders embarked on a mission to create a unique amusement park that would celebrate the thrill and adventure of space travel. Drawing from their backgrounds in aerospace, technology, and entertainment, they set out to design a park that would be both educational and exhilarating.

Vision

At THE NEXUS, we strive to blend cutting-edge technology with creative storytelling to transport you beyond the stars. Our park features state-of-the-art attractions, interactive exhibits, and educational programs that celebrate the marvels of the cosmos. From the thrill of piloting your own spacecraft to the serene beauty of a simulated moonwalk, every corner of our park is designed to inspire and educate.

Mission

Our mission is to foster a sense of wonder and adventure, encouraging guests of all ages to dream big and explore the unknown. We are committed to promoting STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) education through engaging and entertaining experiences, sparking a lifelong passion for learning and discovery.

Awards

Outstanding Achievement in Theme Park Design: THE NEXUS received this coveted award for our groundbreaking use of immersive technology and storytelling that sets new standards in the industry.

Innovation in Technology: The Nexus was celebrated for our pioneering use of augmented reality (AR) and virtual reality (VR) to enhance guest experiences, making space exploration more accessible and thrilling than ever before.

Our Outreach Program

See some of the community outreach projects and endeavours run by the park.

Astronaut Training Camp

Experience the life of an astronaut with our Astronaut Training Camp. Participants undergo simulated space missions, zero-gravity challenges, and team-building exercises designed to inspire the next generation of space explorers

Cosmic Classroom

Join our Cosmic Classroom program and ignite young minds with hands-on space science activities, interactive workshops, and engaging lectures by real astronauts and scientists. Perfect for schools and youth groups!

Starry Night Observations

Explore the night sky with our Starry Night Observations program. Use state-of-the-art telescopes to view planets, stars, and distant galaxies, guided by expert astronomers who share their knowledge and passion for the cosmos.

FAQs

What are the park's operating hours?

The park is open daily from 10 AM to 8 PM. Extended hours are available during holidays and special events.

Are there any height or age restrictions for the rides?

Yes, some rides have height and age restrictions for safety reasons. Please check individual ride details on our website or at the park entrance.

Can I bring my own food and drinks into the park?

Outside food and drinks are not permitted, but we offer a variety of dining options to suit all tastes and dietary needs.

Is the park accessible for guests with disabilities?

Yes, our park is fully accessible, with wheelchair-friendly paths and special accommodations available for guests with disabilities.

Do you offer group discounts or special packages?

Yes, we offer group discounts and special packages for large parties, corporate events, and school trips. Please contact our customer service for more details.
